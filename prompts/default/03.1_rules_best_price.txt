## Rules specific to best_price
- Prefer more, smaller search groups to maximize carrier competition
- Pair long-haul flights as round-trips — a round-trip is dramatically cheaper than two one-ways
- Detach cheap short hops that would inflate the fare basis of expensive long-haul tickets
- Split at alliance/carrier boundaries so each ticket can be priced independently
- When mixing cabin classes, separate them into different tickets to avoid fare contamination
- Look for hidden open-jaw opportunities that price the same as round-trips
